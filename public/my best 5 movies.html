Top Movies of All Time

My Best 3 Movies

Spirited Away

I love this movie because of its beautiful animation, imaginative world, and meaningful story about courage and personal growth.

Ex Machina

This film stands out to me for its intelligent science fiction, thought-provoking themes about artificial intelligence, and suspenseful storytelling.

Drive

I enjoy its unique visual style, memorable soundtrack, and calm yet intense atmosphere, along with its strong performances.
